Pounded Yam And Vegetable Soup Recipe

Pounded Yam and Vegetable Soup are popular African dishes served together.Pounded Yam is similar to mashed potatoes but heavier while Vegetable Soup is made with meats and vegetables.They are very easy dishes to prepare

Pounded Yam

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Yam powder
  • 2 cups of water

    • Directions

    1. Bring 2 cups of water to boil on a stove top. Use a 2 Qt pot or larger
    2. While still on the stove top add the yam powder and mix it together using a wooden spoon
    3. Keep mixing until it becomes smooth and thick
    4. Leave to simmer on heat for 3 minutes

    Note:The entire process takes about 7-10 minutes

    Vegetable Soup

    Ingredients

    • 2 lbs of Pumpkin Leaves
    • 1 lb of Water Leaves
    • 1.5 lbs of beef
    • Pepper, Salt and ground Crayfish: to taste
    • 1 cup of Palm Oil
    • Onions

    Directions

    1. Cut meat in cube like shape and boil in little water with onions and seasoning for 20 minutes
    2. Wash throughly and cut Pumpkin and Water leaves into tiny pieces and place in a strain for water to drain out
    3. Once meat is tender, add 1 cup of Palm Oil, Pepper, and Ground Crayfish and allow to boil for 7 minutes
    4. Now add the Pumpkin and Water leaves and salt to taste
    5. Stir contents very well and turn off heat.
    6. Allow to stand for 5 minutes

    Now serve the dishes together!!!
